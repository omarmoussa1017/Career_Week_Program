Why did we make this program

Dealing between people is the secret of life and the harmony of society yields progress, but now, with the development and the huge and rapid amount of information, the search for this harmony has become difficult, but the most difficult in our time is the ability to harmonize with oneself and get to know it more to have science and practical knowledge of yourself in terms of your professional inclinations, values and skills to own yourself to own your future and your success.Ahmed Mostafa, Dr. Ahmed Mostafa did the first course of the training program in November 2014 in Cairo governorate, but the desire to spread science and expand to make everyone aware of himself to harmonize with his place and be able to identify The doctor presented the training program in several governorates to reach young people seeking for themselves from these governorates Sohag, Cairo, Sharqiya, Alexandria, Beheira, Assiut, Aswan and Qena this was the beginning of the launch of more than a thousand young men and women in identifying their professional inclinations and knowing the engine of their professional inclinations is not only scientific knowledge, it is a deep practical application that reaches inside the self and the human mind the professional tendency is to find out and answer why, and the answer to this question may cost some annihilation His life is in a job that does not suit him, with
